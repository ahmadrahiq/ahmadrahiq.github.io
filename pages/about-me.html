What is Classical Homoeopathy?

Classical Homoeopathy is a holistic therapy. The individual is considered as a whole and symptoms from the body, mind, and spirit are considered when choosing a Homoeopathic remedy. Homoeopathy takes into consideration a person's environment and lifestyle, providing individualized treatment for the person. Classical Homoeopathy most closely matches the totality of the individual's symptoms and personality with the Homoeopathic remedy in order to stimulate the body's own natural healing response. In classical Homoeopathy, the use of a single remedy at a time is a basic principle.

I always try the same principle for patient diagnose, determine the nature of the illness, remedy selection, doses administration, case management and followup, as well as in every step of the treatment process of an individual patient.

I also apply Dr. Rajan Sankaran’s Sensation Method in complex cases.
